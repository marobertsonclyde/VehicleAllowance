# Employee Guide — Vehicle Allowance Program

## What Is the Vehicle Allowance?

Instead of a company vehicle, eligible employees may opt into a monthly cash allowance to cover the cost of using their personal vehicle for work. The allowance is paid monthly and requires you to maintain a qualifying personal vehicle and current insurance coverage.

---

## Am I Eligible?

You are eligible if your job title is on the approved list (CCI SLT, CCI MLT, select manager and construction roles). The Vehicle Allowance portal will confirm your eligibility automatically when you sign in.

If you believe you are eligible but the system says otherwise, contact the Equipment Department.

---

## Applying for the Vehicle Allowance

### Step 1 — Sign In and Check Eligibility

1. Open the Vehicle Allowance portal and sign in with your Clyde Microsoft account.
2. The **Home** screen shows your current status. If you have no active allowance or open application, select **Apply for Vehicle Allowance**.
3. The **Eligibility** screen confirms your job title and allowance tier. If eligible, select **Start Application**.

### Step 2 — Enter Vehicle Details

1. Enter your vehicle's **VIN** (17 characters, found on the driver-side dashboard or door jamb sticker).
2. Enter the **model year**, **make**, and **model**.
3. Upload your **Window Sticker** (the Monroney label from the dealership). The system will read the sticker automatically and fill in the MSRP and powertrain type.
4. Review the extracted values. If anything looks wrong, correct it manually before continuing.
5. If your vehicle is electric or plug-in hybrid, the **Electric Vehicle** option will be detected automatically. Confirm it is correct — EV recipients receive a separate $330/month charging allowance.

> **Tip:** The window sticker is required for new applications. It is the manufacturer's original window sticker, not a dealer quote or invoice.

### Step 3 — Select Your Allowance Level

The system shows the allowance levels your vehicle qualifies for based on its MSRP. Select the appropriate level. Monthly amounts are shown for each level.

| Level | Minimum MSRP | Monthly Allowance |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A | $72,000 | $1,820/mo |
| B | $61,000 | $1,550/mo |
| C | $47,000 | $1,300/mo |
| D | $44,000 | $1,220/mo |

> Levels and amounts are reviewed annually by leadership. Your rate is locked at the time your application is approved.

### Step 4 — Upload Insurance Documents

The program requires proof of insurance meeting minimum coverage requirements. The **Insurance Documents** screen shows a checklist of required documents.

**Required documents:**

| Document | Minimum Coverage Required |
|----------|--------------------------|
| Auto Declaration Page | CSL ≥ $500K, OR BI ≥ $250K/$500K + PD ≥ $100K |
| Umbrella Declaration Page | ≥ $1M (Additional Insured) or ≥ $2M (Additional Interest) |

**For each document:**
1. Select **Upload** next to the checklist item.
2. Choose your file (PDF or image).
3. The system reads the document automatically and displays the extracted values — carrier name, policy number, expiration date, and coverage limits.
4. Review the extracted values. A green checkmark means the coverage meets requirements. A warning means a limit is below the minimum or the value could not be read.
5. If values are incorrect, the Equipment Leader will review manually — you do not need to re-upload unless asked.

> **Important:** Your insurance endorsements must name the correct Clyde subsidiary as Additional Insured or Additional Interest. Contact your insurance agent if you are unsure which entity to list — the Equipment Department can provide the exact endorsement language.

### Step 5 — Review and Submit

1. The **Review & Submit** screen shows a complete summary of your application.
2. Read the attestation statement and check the box to confirm the information is accurate.
3. Select **Submit Application**.

You will receive a Teams message confirming your submission. Your application moves into the review queue.

---

## What Happens After You Submit?

Your application goes through the following review stages. You can track progress on the **Status** screen in the portal.

```
Submitted → AI Review → Equipment Leader Review → Director Review → Active
```

- **AI Review** — the system validates your insurance limits and vehicle details automatically (usually completes within a few minutes).
- **Equipment Leader Review** — the Equipment Leader reviews your application, typically within 2–3 business days.
- **Director Review** — the Director of Equipment gives final approval.

You will receive a Teams message at each stage when action is taken, including if your application is returned for corrections or rejected.

### If Your Application Is Returned

If the Equipment Leader returns your application, you will receive a Teams message explaining what needs to be corrected. Sign back into the portal, make the requested changes (typically re-uploading a document), and resubmit.

### Effective Date

Your allowance starts on the **1st of the month** based on when all your documents were received:

- Documents received on or before the **15th** → allowance starts the **1st of the current month**
- Documents received on the **16th or later** → allowance starts the **1st of the following month**

## Renewing Your Insurance

Your insurance policies must remain current throughout your enrollment. The system will remind you automatically as expiration dates approach.

**Reminder schedule:**
- 30 days before expiration — first reminder via Teams
- 14 days before expiration — second reminder
- 7 days before expiration — final reminder
- On expiration date — urgent notice to you and the Equipment Leader

**To upload a renewal:**
1. Open the portal and go to **Insurance Renewal** (visible from the Home screen when a policy is expiring).
2. Upload your new declaration page.
3. The system reads the new expiration date and coverage limits automatically.

> **Grace period:** If your policy expires without a renewal on file, you have a limited grace period to upload. If no renewal is received within the grace period, your allowance will be suspended automatically. Contact the Equipment Department immediately if you are unable to renew in time.

---

## Opting Out

If you decide to return to a company vehicle, you can opt out at any time.

1. From the **Home** screen, select **Opt Out of Vehicle Allowance**.
2. Review the summary of what opting out means.
3. Confirm by selecting **Submit Opt-Out Request**.

The Equipment Department will contact you to confirm your termination date and coordinate company vehicle assignment. Your allowance continues until the Equipment Leader processes the termination.

---

## Questions?

Use the **Help** button in the portal to open the Vehicle Allowance Assistant, which can answer questions about the program, your application status, and document requirements. For anything the assistant cannot answer, contact the Equipment Department directly.
