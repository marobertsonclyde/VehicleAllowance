# Subsidiary Brand Reference

Use this reference when building output specifically branded to a Clyde Companies subsidiary. Each subsidiary inherits the parent brand's typography rules (Futura > Open Sans > Trebuchet MS) and general layout principles, but has its own color palette, accent stripe colors, brand voice, tagline, and email signature styling.

When a deliverable is for the parent company (Clyde Companies / CAS), use the core palette in the main SKILL.md. When it's for a specific subsidiary, use the overrides below.

## HomeBuilt Hardware and Design (HBL)

**Internal color**: #395276 (Navy/Steel Blue)

**Brand guidelines date**: September 2025 (designed by BCG Design Studios)

**Brand voice**: Practical and inspiring. Built for "The Working Pro" and "The Hands-On Improver" — people defined by conviction, creativity, and curiosity, not just trade or title. Warm, direct, human. Sample copy tone: "You're a builder. A fixer. A maker. And we're here to back you up."

**Mission**: To redesign the home improvement experience around all builders — weekend warriors to weekday pros — combining real service, thoughtful space, and trusted support to make every project feel doable, valued, and worth doing right.

**Vision**: To become the preferred, most trusted, and most innovative service and experience in home and hardware, chosen by builders of every kind.

**Web**: homebuilt.com

**Color palette** (Triadic Color Scheme — accessibility-compliant):

*Primary Colors*:

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK |
|------|-----|-----|------|
| White Stucco | #f3f1ed | 243, 241, 237 | 3, 3, 5, 0 |
| Tan Concrete | #cec9bd | 206, 201, 189 | 19, 17, 24, 0 |
| Cast Iron | #393f46 | 57, 63, 70 | 74, 64, 54, 44 |
| Slate Blue | #395276 | 57, 82, 118 | 85, 68, 32, 15 |

*Secondary Colors*:

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK |
|------|-----|-----|------|
| Glass Blue | #768baa | 118, 139, 170 | 58, 40, 20, 0 |
| Neon Green | #b6fa54 | 243, 241, 237 | 3, 3, 5, 0 |
| Lumber Brown | #dca880 | 220, 168, 128 | 13, 36, 52, 0 |

**Color roles**:
- Slate Blue (#395276): emotional anchor — calm, trust, clarity; dominant brand color
- Neon Green (#b6fa54): accent only — acts like a jobsite highlighter or directional tape; use sparingly for callouts, accents, visual cues
- Cast Iron (#393f46): dark text and structural elements
- Lumber Brown (#dca880): warmth, craftsmanship, materiality
- White Stucco / Tan Concrete: backgrounds and neutral surfaces

**Note**: This is a triadic color scheme. The neon green should never dominate — it's always a signal, not a background. Proportion: Cast Iron and Tan Concrete as dominant neutrals; Slate Blue as brand anchor; Neon Green as sparing accent.

**Typography**:
- Primary font: **Montserrat** (Google Font, free)
- Montserrat Bold: titles and headlines only
- Montserrat Regular: body copy
- Text hierarchy:
  - Heading 01: Montserrat Bold (largest)
  - Heading 02: Montserrat Bold
  - Paragraph Title: Montserrat Bold
  - Eyebrow Title: Montserrat Bold, ALL CAPS
  - Body text: Montserrat Regular
- Text on dark backgrounds: white; accents/callouts in Neon Green
- Text on white backgrounds: Slate Blue (#395276)

**Note**: HomeBuilt uses Montserrat, NOT Futura/Open Sans/Trebuchet. This is completely different from every other Clyde subsidiary's font stack.

**Logo**:
- Logo mark: layered vertical lines forming a building/house silhouette — evokes construction phases, structural strength, and builder community
- Full name lockup: "HOME BUILT / HARDWARE & DESIGN" with increased letter spacing on the "HARDWARE & DESIGN" tagline line
- Primary logo (full color): Neon Green mark + white wordtype on Slate Blue, or Neon Green mark + dark wordtype on light backgrounds
- Secondary logo (one-color): dark/charcoal version on light backgrounds
- Vertical version also available
- App icon: circular badge with Neon Green mark on Slate Blue background
- Clear space: minimum equal to the height of the capital letter "E" on all sides
- Minimum size: 200px wide (digital); 89px height minimum

**Co-branding**: When co-branding with Sunpro (or other partners), use HomeBuilt logo + "+" symbol + partner logo, all properly scaled and aligned on a shared grid.

**Unique design elements**:
- Slate Blue vertical left-edge stripe runs the full height on all branded collateral (thin chartreuse/neon green line, then Slate Blue block)
- Store exterior concept: exposed concrete walls, pitched roofline, natural wood framing, expansive glass windows
- Logo mark also appears engraved/routed in natural wood as a texture element
- Photography style: authentic (natural light, real settings), contextual (products in use), grounded (community/candid moments)
